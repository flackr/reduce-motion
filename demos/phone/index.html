Demo

This demo is based on the typical scroll linked animations present on many product pages such as the iPhone 13 Pro or Pixel 6 Pro product pages.

Ideally such pages would listen to the prefers-reduced-motion media query and provide a reduced motion version of the page, but in the absence of such developer provided versions, this is an attempt to see what could be done automatically to help.
